Introducing Luzerge: Website Monitoring Built for Philippine SMEs

Luzerge Team March 12, 2026 3 min read

If you run a business in the Philippines, you've probably experienced this: your website goes down in the middle of the night, and you don't find out until a customer messages you on Facebook the next morning. Or your SSL certificate expires without warning, and suddenly Google Chrome is telling visitors your site is "Not Secure." By the time you notice, you've already lost sales and trust.

We built Luzerge because we believe every Filipino business deserves to know the moment something goes wrong with their website — without paying enterprise prices in US dollars to find out.

The Problem: Monitoring Tools Weren't Built for Us

Most website monitoring tools on the market today are built for Silicon Valley startups and large enterprises. They're priced in US dollars, designed for DevOps engineers, and packed with features that a bakery owner in Cebu or a resort operator in Palawan will never need. The result? Philippine SMEs either pay too much for tools they barely use, or they go without monitoring entirely and hope for the best.

Consider the math. A popular international monitoring service charges $29/month for basic uptime monitoring. At current exchange rates, that's over ₱1,600 per month — just to know if your website is up or down. For a small business running on tight margins, that's a hard expense to justify. So most don't. And when their site goes down or their certificate expires, they find out the hard way.

We knew there had to be a better approach. Philippine businesses need monitoring that's affordable, simple, and built for their reality — not a stripped-down version of an American enterprise tool.

What Luzerge Offers

Luzerge is a website monitoring and CDN management platform designed from the ground up for Philippine businesses. Here's what you get:

Uptime Monitoring with 60-Second Checks

We check your website every 60 seconds from multiple locations around the world. The moment your site goes down, you get an alert — by email, SMS, or webhook. No more finding out from angry customers. You'll know about outages within a minute, giving you time to contact your hosting provider or fix the issue before most visitors even notice.

SSL Certificate Tracking

An expired SSL certificate doesn't just trigger browser warnings — it destroys customer trust and can expose sensitive data. Luzerge monitors your SSL certificates and alerts you days before they expire, so you never get caught off guard. We also check for configuration issues like mixed content, weak cipher suites, and certificate chain problems.

CDN and Cloudflare Management

Cloudflare is one of the best free tools available to Philippine businesses — it speeds up your site, protects against DDoS attacks, and provides a free SSL certificate. But managing Cloudflare settings can be confusing. Luzerge integrates directly with Cloudflare, giving you a simplified dashboard to manage DNS records, security settings, caching rules, and page rules without needing to learn Cloudflare's complex interface.

Performance Analytics

Speed matters. A site that loads in 5 seconds instead of 2 loses nearly half its visitors. Luzerge tracks your website's response times over days, weeks, and months, so you can spot performance trends and fix slowdowns before they affect your bottom line. You'll see exactly how fast your site loads from different regions and identify bottlenecks holding you back.

Free Website Scanner

Not sure where you stand? Our free website scanner analyzes your domain's Cloudflare status, DNS configuration, SSL setup, and hosting details in seconds. No signup required. It's the fastest way to understand your site's current security and performance posture.

How We're Different

There are plenty of monitoring tools out there. Here's why Luzerge is different:

  • Priced in Philippine pesos. No exchange rate surprises. No hidden fees in dollars. You know exactly what you're paying, and it's a fraction of what international tools charge.
  • Built on Cloudflare's global network. We leverage Cloudflare's infrastructure — the same network used by Fortune 500 companies — to deliver enterprise-grade reliability at SME-friendly prices.
  • Designed for business owners, not DevOps engineers. You shouldn't need a computer science degree to monitor your website. Our dashboard is clean, simple, and tells you exactly what you need to know in plain language.
  • Local support from Cebu City. When you need help, you're talking to a real team based in the Philippines. We understand Philippine hosting providers, local DNS setups, and the specific challenges Filipino businesses face online. No chatbots, no timezone gaps, no language barriers.
We didn't build Luzerge for everyone. We built it for the sari-sari store owner who just launched an online ordering site, the Cebu resort that depends on bookings through their website, and the Manila startup that needs enterprise tools on a bootstrap budget.

Simple, Transparent Pricing

Our pricing is straightforward because we believe monitoring should be accessible to every business, regardless of size:

  • Free plan: Monitor 1 domain with uptime checks and SSL tracking. No credit card required. No trial period. Free forever.
  • Managed plans from ₱99/month: Additional domains, Cloudflare CDN management, performance analytics, priority alerts, and dedicated support.

We accept the payment methods Philippine businesses actually use: GCash, Maya, bank transfer, and PayPal. No international credit card required.

Our Mission

The internet doesn't care whether you're a multinational corporation or a one-person business in Davao. Downtime costs you customers either way. An expired SSL certificate scares away visitors whether you have 100 of them or 100,000.

Our mission is simple: make enterprise-grade website monitoring accessible to every Filipino business. We believe that the tools used to protect million-dollar websites should be available to every entrepreneur, freelancer, and SME in the Philippines — at a price that makes sense for the local market.

Whether you're running a personal portfolio, an e-commerce store, or a growing SaaS product, Luzerge watches your site around the clock so you can focus on what matters most — running your business.
