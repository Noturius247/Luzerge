Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Luzerge CDN.

What is a CDN and why do I need one?

A CDN (Content Delivery Network) stores copies of your website at servers around the world. When someone visits your site, they load it from the nearest server instead of your origin — making it significantly faster. It also protects against DDoS attacks and reduces your server load.

Do I need a Cloudflare account?

No. We handle all the Cloudflare configuration for you. Just submit your domain through our dashboard and our team sets up everything — zone configuration, cache rules, SSL, and DDoS protection.

How fast will my website be after using Luzerge CDN?

Most websites see a 40–80% improvement in load time. Static assets (images, CSS, JavaScript) are served from the nearest edge location with under 50ms latency. The exact improvement depends on your current setup and where your visitors are located.

Can I purge the cache when I update my website?

Yes! Your dashboard has a one-click cache purge. You can clear everything or specific URLs. Changes propagate globally within seconds.

Will it work with my existing hosting?

Yes. Luzerge CDN works as a layer in front of any hosting provider — shared hosting, VPS, dedicated servers, or cloud platforms. Your existing setup stays the same.

How long does setup take?

Submit your domain and our team typically configures everything within 24 hours. You'll see your domain status change to "Active" in your dashboard once it's ready.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your first month is completely free — no payment needed upfront. After that, cancel anytime with no lock-in contracts. We'll remove the CDN configuration and your site reverts to its original setup.

How do I pay?

We accept GCash, Maya, bank transfer (BDO/BPI/UnionBank), and PayPal. Monthly billing — pay when you're satisfied each month.
