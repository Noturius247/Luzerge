About Luzerge

Website monitoring built for Philippine businesses

The Problem

Most Philippine SMEs struggle with slow, unreliable websites. Cloudflare offers powerful (and free) tools to fix this — global caching, DDoS protection, SSL — but setting it up is intimidating. Monitoring tools that could help are priced in USD, built for enterprises, and assume technical expertise that many small business owners don't have.

The result: Filipino businesses lose customers to slow load times and downtime they don't even know about.

Our Solution

Luzerge brings enterprise-grade website monitoring to Philippine SMEs at prices that make sense. One dashboard to monitor uptime, manage your CDN cache, track SSL certificates, view analytics, and protect against threats — all powered by Cloudflare's global network.

  • Free website scanner — instantly check any domain's Cloudflare, DNS, hosting, and nameserver setup
  • Uptime monitoring — get alerted before your customers notice downtime
  • One-click cache purge — update your site and see changes immediately worldwide
  • Real-time analytics — requests, bandwidth, threats, and cache performance
  • Priced in pesos — starting free, managed plans from just ₱99/month

Why Cebu

Luzerge is built in Cebu City — the Philippines' fastest-growing tech hub. We understand the local market because we're part of it. But our infrastructure is global: Cloudflare's 300+ edge locations for speed, Supabase in Singapore for data, and GitHub for reliable deployments.

Local support, global performance.
